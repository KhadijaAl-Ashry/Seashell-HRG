About Seashell ...

It is a promising company in establishing restaurants and cafes. Seashell was established in Hurghada in 2024 as the beginning of a group of restaurants that are expected to be established. You can imagine the beauty of the location and the charm of the view in front of the waters of the Red Sea, which will give you a feeling of comfort and tranquility.

SeaShell is the starting point for a group of awaited projects across Egypt.
